SAT Workshops

Each four-hour workshop is comprehensive and designed to clarify the critical thinking and writing components of the SAT.

Click on a date in the chart below to find out more information. You may also observe a workshop to see if it fits your needs!

Students Receive

  • Materials
  • Explanation of the multiple choice and essay portions of the test
  • Opportunity to study live examples of actual graded student essays
  • Tips for focusing on important factors for success
  • Practice with question/prompt preparation in test-taking mode
  • Evaluation of a practice essay student writes after the workshop!
